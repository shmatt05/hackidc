About Flashbook

Flashbook is a vacation booking service for the free spirited.

By collecting your lifestyle preferences, we locate the perfect vacation that fits your needs, and book it immediately. Our algorithm lets you seize the rare opportunities where traveling ticket prices suddenly drop without notice, and are available for only seconds or less. All you need to do is fill in your preferences, and we do the rest of the work for you. Once we find a matching ticket, we will go through the booking process for you, and send you your booked itinerary. Happy Travelling!
